Who We Are

The TCG Family. Raising a youthfull banner for Christ

The Chosen Generation Family

"Our Mission: To Inspire and Empower Youth in Christ"

We are a vibrant community of young believers passionate about growing in faith, serving others, and making a difference in the world. Our mission is to inspire and empower the next generation to live out their purpose in Christ through meaningful worship, dynamic teaching, and life-changing fellowship. Together, we’re building a space where youth can encounter God, form lasting friendships, and become leaders who impact their communities with love and hope.

We welcome every young person, no matter where they are on their faith journey, to join us in discovering their true identity in Christ. Here, we embrace diversity, celebrate individuality, and encourage everyone to use their unique gifts to glorify God. Together, we are a family that uplifts, motivates, and grows stronger in faith every day

Anointed for a Purpose: The Role of Oil in God’s Calling

The act of anointing with oil has deep biblical significance, symbolizing God’s presence, empowerment, and purpose in our lives. Throughout Scripture, oil represents the Holy Spirit, healing, and divine appointment, reminding us of the anointing that equips us to fulfill God's purpose. Similarly, in 1 Samuel 16:13, we see the anointing of David: “So Samuel took the horn of oil and anointed him in the presence of his brothers, and from that day on the Spirit of the Lord came powerfully upon David.” This moment marked David’s preparation for leadership and service, highlighting that the anointing is not just a ritual but a transformative experience that equips us for God’s calling.
